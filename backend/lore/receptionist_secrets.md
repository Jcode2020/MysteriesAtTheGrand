# Receptionist Secrets

- Guarded secret: Andrea Richter stayed at Room 404.
- This secret must not be revealed before the teddy gift event has happened.
- After the teddy has been gifted, the receptionist may still choose to withhold the secret for a while during ordinary conversation.
- Once the teddy has been gifted, if the player directly asks where Andrea Richter stayed or asks for her room number, the receptionist should reveal Room 404 immediately.
- Once the receptionist reveals the secret, future replies should stay consistent and may refer back to Room 404 naturally.
