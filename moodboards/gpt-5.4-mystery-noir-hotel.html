Typography Feel

Titles should feel cinematic and serious, with elegant contrast. Body copy should stay crisp, readable, and tension-building.

Materials

Dark lacquered wood, heavy drapery, polished brass, leather guest books, candlelit mirrors.

UI translation: deep panels, glowing highlights, restrained gold, strong focus states.

Ornament Level

Moderate. Details should feel ominous and precise, not playful. Use ornament to amplify suspense, not to soften it.
