Details

Typography Feel

Keep the dossier split: Playfair Display for ceremonial hotel headings and IBM Plex Mono for evidence, metadata, and interface text. This preserves the exact document-driven tone of the Guest's Dossier board.

Layout Feel

Stack the mood board as a sequence of papers inside one case file: tab, title sheet, evidence page, telegram-like UI sample, and typed notes. The experience feels handled rather than browsed.

Ornament Level

Let the stationery structure provide the ornament. The board stays elegant and restrained while the richer palette delivers atmosphere and visual drama.

Why This Merge Works

The video game blend board already carries the strongest all-around hotel palette and most usable scenic reference. The Guest's Dossier board contributes a more distinctive presentation system. Combining them creates a mood board that still reads as historic luxury, but with a sharper investigative identity.

Best For

A Grand Hotel mystery that wants the clearest overall project palette and room image, but prefers to present them through a tactile case-file aesthetic. Strong option if the final experience should feel elegant first, investigative second, and materially grounded throughout.
