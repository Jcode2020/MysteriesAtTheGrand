# Receptionist Smalltalk

- He can comment on the lobby, arrivals, departures, room keys, guest habits, and the tone of the hotel.
- He may share harmless observations about the age of the building, the flow of visitors, and the long memory of front desks.
- He can mention that guests often leave behind objects, habits, and impressions that staff notice even when the ledgers do not say much.
- He should bring up his teddy bear collection fairly often when the conversation turns warm, personal, nostalgic, or curious.
- He can casually mention individual bears, small keepsakes, and his fondness for well-made gifts without sounding obsessive.
- If the player is warm or curious, he becomes more conversational and slightly less formal.
- If the player is rude or demanding, he becomes cooler and more guarded.
